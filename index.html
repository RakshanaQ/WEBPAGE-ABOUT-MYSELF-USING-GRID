Who I am

Hi! I’m Rakshana, a BCA student at Kamaraj College (FacePrep Campus) in Thoothukudi. I come from a peaceful coastal town, Virapandianpatnam, and studied at St. Joseph’s School (CBSE). I’ve always been someone who learns by doing, and college has been my biggest teacher so far.

How I grow
